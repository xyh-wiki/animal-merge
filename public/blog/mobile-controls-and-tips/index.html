# Mobile Controls and Tips for Animal Merge Evolution Animal Merge Evolution works great on phones and tablets. Here are some tips to make your mobile experience smoother. ## Basic Mobile Controls - Swipe on the board to move all animals: - Swipe up → move up - Swipe down → move down - Swipe left → move left - Swipe right → move right - The page is configured to **not scroll** while you are swiping on the board. ## Turn Off Unnecessary System Gestures On some devices: - System gestures (home bar, back swipe) can interfere with horizontal swipes. - Consider enabling **“gesture protection“** or playing in landscape mode if available. ## Play in Short Sessions - Daily Challenge is perfect for quick mobile sessions. - Classic mode can also be played in short bursts; the game doesn’t require long continuous focus. ## Battery and Data Usage - The game is **light-weight** and runs fully in the browser. - There is no heavy download; graphics are mostly emojis and simple layouts. With good swiping habits and a stable internet connection, mobile play can be just as strong as desktop play. [Play on your phone](https://animal-merge.xyh.wiki/)
