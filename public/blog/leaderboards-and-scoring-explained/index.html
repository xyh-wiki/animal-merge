# Leaderboards and Scoring in Animal Merge Evolution This article explains how scoring works and how the leaderboards decide your ranking. ## How Score Is Calculated While the internal formula can change, the basic idea is: - Each merge gives you points based on the **level** of the resulting animal. - Higher-level merges are worth significantly more points. - Long chains of merges in a single move are usually very powerful. ## What the Leaderboards Show There are two main leaderboards: 1. **Classic – All time** - Shows your best runs in Classic mode. - Sorted by score, then by moves. 2. **Daily challenge – Today** - Shows runs played in Daily mode **for today’s date**. Each entry usually includes: - Rank (#1, #2, #3…) - Score - Highest animal name and emoji - Number of moves used ## How to Save a Run - Finish your game or reach a point where you are satisfied. - Click **“New Game”**. - The game will automatically save your current run into the appropriate leaderboard. ## Improving Your Ranking - Focus on **playing cleanly**, not just quickly. - Avoid wasting moves that don’t create merges. - In Daily mode, try again later in the day if you discover a better strategy.
