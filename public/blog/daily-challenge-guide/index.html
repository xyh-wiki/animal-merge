# Daily Challenge Guide – Make the Most of Today’s Board Animal Merge Evolution includes a Daily Challenge mode that gives you a fresh board every day and a dedicated daily leaderboard. ## How Daily Challenge Works - Each day has a unique **starting layout** and **random seed**. - All players share the same conditions on that day. - Your best finished run is recorded in the **daily leaderboard**. ## Why Play Daily Mode? - Short sessions with a clear goal. - Fair comparison with other players on the same board. - Great for warming up and testing new strategies. ## Tips for Better Daily Scores 1. **Play Two or Three Warm-Up Runs** Use early runs to understand the “shape“ of the board and how it tends to fill. 2. **Adapt, Don’t Force Your Classic Strategy** Some daily boards favor vertical merges, others horizontal. Adjust your main corner if needed. 3. **Use Undo Only When Necessary** Undo is still limited in Daily. Save it for moves that: - Destroy your main stacking pattern - Accidentally fill the board with low-level animals 4. **Know When to Reset** If you make a very early mistake that ruins your corner, it may be faster to restart. ## How Scores Are Ranked - Higher **score** is better. - If two scores are equal, fewer **moves** is better. - The highest **animal level** is also displayed as a quick way to see strong runs.
