# Animal Merge Evolution – FAQ and Troubleshooting This page answers common questions about Animal Merge Evolution and helps you fix simple issues. ## The Game Does Not Load - Make sure you are using a modern browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox, Safari). - Check your network connection and try refreshing the page. - If you have aggressive ad-block or script-block extensions, try disabling them for this site. ## Swipes Are Not Detected on Mobile - Swipe directly on the board area, not on the page background. - Try shorter, more deliberate swipes. - Make sure your browser is not intercepting gestures for “back” or “refresh“. ## The Board Stopped Moving - If there are **no valid merges left**, the game will end with a “Game Over“ message. - Click **“New Game”** to start again. - Use Undo early if you feel a move created a bad board state. ## My Score Did Not Save - Scores are saved when you click **“New Game”** after a run. - Leaderboards are stored locally in your browser using `localStorage`. - Clearing your browser data will also clear your local records. ## Is the Game Free? Yes. Animal Merge Evolution is free to play in your browser. In the future, small ads may be added to support hosting costs. [Back to the main game](https://animal-merge.xyh.wiki/)
