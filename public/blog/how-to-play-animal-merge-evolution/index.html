# How to Play Animal Merge Evolution – Beginner’s Guide Animal Merge Evolution is a relaxing browser puzzle game where you swipe to move cute animals and merge them into higher evolution levels. This guide walks you through the basic rules, controls, and simple strategies. ## Basic Rules - All animals on the board move in the direction you choose (up, down, left, or right). - When two animals of the same level collide, they merge into the next evolution. - Each merge gives you score and brings you closer to the final animal (Dragon and beyond). - The game is over when there are no valid moves left on the board. ## Controls on Desktop - Use the **arrow keys** (↑ ↓ ← →) to move animals. - You don’t need to click the board first – global keyboard control is enabled. - You can also use **W/A/S/D** if your browser captures them as arrow keys. ## Controls on Mobile - Swipe **up, down, left, right** directly on the board. - The page will not scroll while you are swiping. - Short, clean swipes are easier for the game to detect. ## Game Modes - **Classic Mode** – standard merge puzzle with no time limit. - **Daily Mode** – a fresh challenge every day, with its own daily leaderboard. - More modes may be added in the future, so check back regularly. ## What Counts as a Good Run? - A run with **high score**, **few moves**, and a **high-level animal**. - Finishing a game and starting a new one will save the result into the leaderboard.
