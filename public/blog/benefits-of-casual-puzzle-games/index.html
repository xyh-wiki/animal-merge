# Why Casual Puzzle Games Like Animal Merge Evolution Are Great for Relaxing Casual puzzle games are more than just time killers. When designed well, they can help you relax, reset your focus, and improve your mood. ## Low Pressure, High Reward - No strict time limits. - Clear goals (reach higher animals, beat your own score). - Frequent small rewards from merges and visual effects. ## Short Play Sessions - You can play for a few minutes during breaks. - Daily Challenges give you a natural start and end point. - There is no need to commit to a long session. ## Gentle Cognitive Training - Planning merges trains short-term planning and pattern recognition. - You practice evaluating options and making efficient decisions. ## Visual and Audio Relaxation - Cute animal emojis and soft animations are less stressful than competitive shooters. - If you play with background music, it can become a calming ritual. Next time you need a short break, try a small run in Animal Merge Evolution instead of scrolling endlessly. [Play a relaxing round](https://animal-merge.xyh.wiki/)
