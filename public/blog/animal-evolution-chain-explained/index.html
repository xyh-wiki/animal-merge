# Animal Evolution Chain Explained One of the most satisfying parts of Animal Merge Evolution is watching animals evolve into stronger and rarer forms. ## From Mouse to Dragon While the exact chain may change in future updates, the idea is: - Start with basic animals like **Mouse** and **Cat** - Merge your way up through medium levels (Rabbit, Fox, etc.) - Reach high-level animals such as **Panda**, **Tiger**, and finally **Dragon** Each step in the chain has: - An emoji - A name - An internal value used for scoring and highlighting ## Why Evolution Matters - Higher-level animals **unlock more score per merge**. - When you reach certain milestones, the game may show special visual effects. - Reaching Dragon or beyond is often used as a personal high-score goal. ## Reading the Evolution Panel On the right side of the game, you can see: - A visual chain of animals connected with arrows. - Each emoji and name shows where it sits in the evolution. Use this panel to plan ahead and decide which merges are most valuable. [See the evolution chain in the game](https://animal-merge.xyh.wiki/)
